Irrational Fear

I'm not sure if I'm clautrophobic or just overdramatic but one thing's for sure is my immense fear for confined spaces.

You would never see me ride an elevator even if I have to go to the 38th floor inside a building. No. Never. I would seriously take the stairs no matter how long and tiring it would take as long as I don't ride the elevator and feel constricted. Not just inside the elevator that I would feel anxious, other places including under ground tunnels as well. That's why I try to avoid taking the MRT and would gladly choose another transportation vehicle anytime.

I don't know why I have this constant fear of being trapped inside a confined place, maybe because I'm over thinking about worst case scenarios wherein I would be trapped but in reality I'm aware that it won't happen.

Bottom line is, I just don't want to feel trapped.
