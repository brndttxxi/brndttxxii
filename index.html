Let's dive into the world of Kpop (bows in korean)
 




BTS also known as Bangtan So Nyeon Dan (which literally translates to BulletProof Boyscouts in english) however they recently debuted into a more cooler name which is "Behind The Scene" tho I like the first one better :-( .So, they're a 7 member hiphop boy group in korea consisting of Jin, J-hope, Jimin, Jungkook, Suga, V, and Rap-monster. They're taking the world by storm since their second full album Wings which peaked at #26 in the Billboard top 200. And that's insane! They're the first ever kpop group who marked the highest ranking chart in the history of kpop. And they also won Top Social Artist at Billboard Music Awards, beating other nominees including Justin Bieber, Selena Gomez, Ariana Grande, and Shawn Mendes.I felt ecstatic when they won that award because they proved that through hard work they can achieve anything. I seriously love them so much!
